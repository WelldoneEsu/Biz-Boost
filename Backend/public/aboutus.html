50k+ SaaS for Nigeria's micro-retailers

Empowering Nigeria’s smallest shops to dream bigger

At BizBoost, we believe small businesses deserve big opportunities. From market stalls and kiosks to mini-marts and local services, Nigeria’s micro-retailers are the heartbeat of our economy — yet many run without the tools they need to grow. We’re here to change that.

Wonderful Story Behind BizBoost

We started BizBoost after seeing how many traders struggled with poor record-keeping, unpredictable stock, and customer debts that were hard to track. Many were working hard but couldn’t prove their business performance to access loans or supplier credit. Our mission is simple: give every small shop owner an easy, affordable tool to run their business like a pro. With BizBoost, you can track sales, manage stock, follow up on debts, and see your true profit — all from one app that works online or offline.

Our Mission

To empower every Nigerian micro and small business owner with simple technology that builds confidence, increases profits, and opens the door to new opportunities.

Our Vision

A future where every shop — no matter how small — has the tools to grow, thrive, and compete in the modern economy.
