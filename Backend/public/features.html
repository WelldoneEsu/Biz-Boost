Grow faster with tools built for your business

BizBoost combines easy-to-use features with the reliability you need to keep your shop running smoothly. Track sales, manage stock, and record customer credit in one place — even without the internet. As you work, BizBoost builds a digital business profile that helps you qualify for loans and supplier deals, turning today’s shop into tomorrow’s success story.

Sales Management

  • Log transactions for cash, transfer, or mobile payments.
  • Apply discounts or special pricing per sale.
  • Track trends and performance over time.
  • Identify and restock best-performing items faster.
  • Link purchases to specific customers for better follow-up.
  • See your profit updated automatically after each sale.
  • Record and adjust for returned products.
  • Accept and log different payment types in one system.
  • Quickly find past transactions for review or correction.

Inventory Management

  • Real-time stock tracking and updates
  • Low stock alerts and automatic reorder notifications
  • Barcode and QR code scanning for quick item entry
  • Identify and restock best-performing items faster.
  • Categorization and tagging of products for easy search
  • Detailed reporting and analytics on inventory usage and trends
  • Multi-location inventory management and transfer tracking

Customer & IOU Management

  • Centralized customer database with contact details and transaction history
  • Easy creation and tracking of IOUs (debt records)
  • Automated reminders for pending and overdue payments
  • Ability to link customers with multiple purchases or services
  • Detailed reporting on outstanding balances and settlements
  • Secure record-keeping with editable and searchable entries
  • Record and adjust for returned products
  • Credit limit settings and monitoring for each customer
  • Payment history tracking with receipts and verification

Delivery Management

  • Centralized database of supplier details and contacts
  • Supplier performance tracking (delivery times, reliability, pricing)
  • Route optimization for faster and cost-effective deliveries
  • Integration with drivers/riders for live tracking
  • Automated delivery notifications via SMS or email
  • Proof of delivery with signature or photo capture
  • Record and adjust for returned products
  • Delivery history and performance analytics
  • Support for multiple delivery options

Supplier Management

  • Centralized database of supplier details and contacts
  • Supplier performance tracking
  • Purchase order creation and management
  • Identify and restock best-performing items faster.
  • Integration of supplier invoices with payment records
  • See your profit updated automatically after each sales.
  • Automated reminders for due payments or contract renewals
  • Comparative analysis of multiple suppliers for better decision-making
  • History of past transactions and supplier communication logs
